With the release of Shadow of the Erdtree, 8 new Weapon categories are added to the already stacked 32 Weapon Categories available in the base game, to a total of 40. This 8 new Weapon categories are: Beast Claws, Hand-to-Hand Arts, Throwing Blades, Backhand Blades, Perfume Bottles, Light Greatswords, Great Katanas, and Thrusting Shields. Learn all about the 8 new weapon types in Shadow of the Erdtree and how to master them! That's not all, the old weapon categories will receive new weapons, allowing players to have an even bigger arsenal at their disposal to traverse through the dangerous Realm of Shadow, in search of Miquella. Shadow of the Erdtree adds a total of 100 new and exciting Weapons to Elden Ring. This page is up to date with all this new content. Players will be able to search and find all Shadow of the Erdtree New Weapons here, as well as to learn about their Stats, Scaling, Requirements, Upgrades, Builds that use them, and much more regarding the new weapons added with the Shadow of the Erdtree DLC! This comprehensive guide will help players optimize their combat strategies and fully utilize the potential of these new weapons.
